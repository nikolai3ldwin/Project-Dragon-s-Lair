Our Mission

Creating a virtual world to connect people and their stories.

Hello!


We are Project Dragon's Lair! This idea inspired while pumping iron in the gym. Highly recommended place to think about your place in the universe. More importantl, this idea was to study learning in a virtual reality environment. It was hypothesized that experience would help people to feel more connected and involved in their learning -- with their peers and their teacher -- and that this would promote better learning in a world that is currently taking shelter from a pandemic. A horrible thought, is what if this happens again? Studies already show that learning was behind, that the general public's mental and emotional health was worsened from the experience, and that people's futures were being impaired.


Project Dragon's Lair sees people as just that. People. People have stories to tell and share. We hope to create a platform where people can learn together, work together, and play together. Help collaborate and build with us to realize this.


Let us know how we can help you to tell your stories and connect with your peers in whichever environment you need. Consultations allow us to help you.
